How to make a sandwich (yum)

Ingridients-

  1. Some bread (availble in almost all kinds of markets)
  2. Some chesse
  3. Cucumber(depends on how many sandwiches you would like to make)
  4. Tomatos(depends on how many sandwiches you would like to make)
  5. origano

How to make the delicious meal

  1. Slice the bread(if it is not sliced)
  2. Put the pan on the stove and put butter on it.
  3. Put two peices of bread on the pan,after 30 seconds flip the bread.
  4. after the bread is cooked put it away to cool and slice the tomatos and the cucumbers into tiny peices.
  5. Now, put 1 peice of bread on the table, sprinkle some tomato peices on it.
  6. Then put a slice of cheese on it.
  7. After that sprinkle some cucumber peices on it.
  8. Then again put a slice of cheese.
  9. Then again sprinkle the cucumber peices and then the tomato peices.
  10. TADDA!! your meal is ready to serve
